WASD - move.
QE - change elevation.
1 - Toggles AO.
2 - Toggles AtmosphericOcclusion.
T - Toggles Textures.
R - Resets 3D texture offset.
Up, Down, Left, Right, Page Up, Page Down - Shift 3D texture offset.

OpenGL 4.3
